About

Hey there! My name is David Wolf, a West Chester University graduate currently in the Philadelphia area. Since graduating, I've spent a bulk of my time furthering my passion in web and mobile application development in the Philadelphia region. Being part of the Philadelphia digital marketing scene has given me opportunities to refine my skills and understanding of website builds and applications.

I'm interested in business intelligence and analytical reporting. I hope to continue my work in conceptualizing, designing, and developing analytical dashboards and refining my understanding of strong user experience practices.

My interest in mobile technologies has expanded my curiosity into beacon/iBeacon technology (iBeacon). Presently I'm working with the Estimote iBeacon Android API, https://github.com/Estimote/Android-SDK, I'm enjoying setting personal beacons around my house and testing what actions I can perform with the beacon technology.

In my spare time I enjoy staying active by running, playing sports, and snowboarding when the season fits. I slapa da bass and enjoy a diverse range of music, so feel free to check out my last.fm profile.

Admittedly a novice, I'm trying to learn the brew trade and enjoy all aspects of beer and brewing, especially drinking it.
